# CAPTURE: Daily Workflow

## Morning (First 15 min)

1. **Open ops-home dashboard** (`localhost:3000/dashboard`)
2. **Check market strip**: Price action, funding rates, smart money moves
3. **Scan knowledge panel** (Cmd+K): Any alerts, notes flagged for review
4. **Review calendar**: Today's tasks, bot status, position check-ins

---

## During the Day (Continuous Capture)

### Knowledge Capture
- **Twitter/X thread worth saving?** → Cmd+K → Paste link + 1-line summary
- **Farcaster insight?** → Note in `~/dojo/knowledge/farcaster/` with date
- **Research deep-dive?** → Create markdown in `~/dojo/knowledge/protocols/[name].md`
- **Random idea?** → Quick note in ops-home (searchable later)

### Position/Wallet Actions
**NEVER interact with wallets without checking the safety panel first.**

- **Before any tx**: Check wallet risk lane (hot/warm/cold)
- **Log position changes**: Update positions panel (protocol, size, entry, thesis)
- **Bot monitoring**: Check Freqtrade status, adjust if needed

### Meta-Skills / Learning
- **Poker hand review?** → `~/dojo/poker/hands/[date].md`
- **DeFi strategy idea?** → `~/dojo/defi-strats/[strategy-name].md`
- **Something clicked?** → Meta-note explaining the pattern

---

## Evening (Review & Process)

1. **Review today's captures**: Cmd+K search, tag/organize anything loose
2. **Update changelog**: Quick bullet of what shipped or learned
3. **Check positions**: Any exits needed? Risks changed?
4. **Plan tomorrow**: Set 1-3 calendar tasks
5. **Spaced repetition** (if enabled): Review flashcards (5 min)

---

## Weekly Review (Sunday, 20 min)

1. **Skim knowledge captures**: Archive, consolidate, or expand important notes
2. **Position retrospective**: What worked? What didn't? Log learnings
3. **Update INTENT.md**: Any shifts in focus, priorities, or architecture?
4. **Clean up dashboard**: Remove stale tasks, update project statuses

---

## Rules

- **Capture first, organize later**: Don't let perfect structure block capture
- **One source of truth**: All knowledge → `~/dojo/knowledge`, all positions → ops-home
- **Cmd+K is your friend**: Fast search > rigid folders
- **Wallet safety > speed**: Always check risk lane before signing
- **No tab chaos**: Dashboard replaces 20 open tabs

---

**This workflow exists to build systematic habits, not add friction. Adapt as needed.**
